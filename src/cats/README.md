# Cat Clicker

An incremental clicker game featuring an adorable cat in a 2D side-view world environment. Pet your cat, earn love and treats, unlock achievements, and watch your cat grow happier!

## Features

- **2D World Environment**: Immersive side-view house interior with furniture, windows, and multiple rooms
- **Cat Interactions**: Pet, click, and play with your cat to earn love
- **Job System**: Work jobs to earn treats, then convert treats to love for upgrades
- **Achievement System**: Unlock milestones and awards as you progress
- **Merit Upgrades**: Spend love on upgrades that boost your earning potential
- **Skills System**: Train skills to unlock new abilities and bonuses
- **Wand Toy**: Interactive wand toy mode for special cat interactions
- **Camera Controls**: Pan around the world to explore different areas

## How to Play

1. **Pet Your Cat**: Click on your cat to earn love
2. **Work Jobs**: Click the job button to earn treats over time
3. **Convert Treats**: Use treats to generate love (your primary currency)
4. **Buy Upgrades**: Spend love on merit upgrades to boost earnings
5. **Unlock Achievements**: Reach milestones to earn rewards
6. **Train Skills**: Level up skills to unlock new abilities

## Game Mechanics

- **Love-per-Interaction**: Base love earned scales with your current love (1% minimum)
- **Energy System**: Cat energy affects interaction rewards
- **Infinite Progression**: All upgrade paths continue indefinitely
- **Achievement Rewards**: Milestones and awards provide permanent bonuses
