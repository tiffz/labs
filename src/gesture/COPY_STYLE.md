# The Gesture Room — copy

Defer to [`docs/USER_COPY_STYLE.md`](../../docs/USER_COPY_STYLE.md) first.

## Voice

- Calm and assured, like a quiet studio or a well-run dining room.
- Short lines. One idea per sentence.
- Product name in UI: **The Gesture Room** (not "Gesture" alone).

## Domain terms

- **Collection** — a linked or uploaded Drive folder of photos.
- **Practice** — timed drawing session (zen mode / the room).
- **Drawn** — user completed a timed slot for that photo (not sketched in-app). Track `sessionCount` per photo for least-drawn ordering.

## Avoid

- “Line of Action” or other product names in UI.
- Em dashes in user-visible strings.
- Asking for tags, subjects, or notes during upload — add tags on a collection card after it lands.
