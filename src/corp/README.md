# Corporate Ladder

A lightweight, emoji-first roguelite about climbing the corporate dungeon. Navigate procedurally generated office floors, manage your stats, and try to reach the top!

## Features

- **Procedural Generation**: Each floor is randomly generated with rooms, corridors, and entities
- **Field of View**: Fog of war reveals areas as you explore
- **Stat Management**: Track productivity, happiness, and reputation
- **Skills System**: Unlock and upgrade skills as you progress
- **Inventory**: Collect items to help you on your journey
- **Game Over Conditions**: Game ends when stats drop too low or you reach the CEO floor

## How to Play

1. **Move**: Use arrow keys or WASD to navigate the dungeon
2. **Explore**: Reveal the map by moving into new areas
3. **Manage Stats**: Keep an eye on productivity, happiness, and reputation
4. **Collect Items**: Pick up items to boost your stats
5. **Reach the Top**: Try to climb as high as possible!

## Game Mechanics

- **Procedural Floors**: Each floor is randomly generated
- **Fog of War**: Only visible areas are shown on the map
- **Stat Decay**: Stats decrease over time - manage them carefully
- **Promotion System**: Reach higher floors to advance your career
